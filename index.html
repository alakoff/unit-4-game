Crystals Collector Game


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given to you at the start of the game. You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number given.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
